Testing OAuth Flow

This page tests the Google OAuth flow from your backend to frontend.

Step 1: Test Backend Connection

Step 2: Start OAuth Flow

Expected Flow:

  1. Click "Start Google OAuth"
  2. You'll be redirected to Google's consent screen
  3. Sign in with your Google account
  4. Google will redirect back to your backend
  5. Backend will redirect to: http://localhost:3000/oauth-callback
  6. You should see a success message and be redirected to the dashboard
